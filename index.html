Who Is This Master?

Given into the desire for power, one man has decided to take on all the world's problems personally. To force peace in the galaxy requires much discipline. He rules the Sith with an iron fist. His intention? Peace throughout the galaxy. The belief that freedom brings peace is a lie. Peace comes from constant control. Controlling mankind but giving into ones emotional tendencies is what allows true peace.

This Man is Scott Adams also known as SithScott
